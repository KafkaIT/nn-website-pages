Tracking your nutrition is one of the most effective ways to stay on top of your health goals.

Small, user-friendly nutrition tracking software can help you monitor your food intake, track nutrients, and keep you accountable.

With the right app, you can gain insights into your eating habits and make adjustments to reach your wellness objectives.

This guide covers eight of the best nutrition tracking tools to consider, including the new and promising app, NoirNourish.

1. NoirNourish

NoirNourish is a new, streamlined nutrition tracking app designed to make tracking simple and accessible. Focused on essential features, NoirNourish provides a minimalist approach, ideal for users who want effective nutrition tracking without overwhelming options. Key features include:

  • Easy meal logging and nutrition breakdown
  • Customizable goals for macronutrient and calorie intake
  • User-friendly interface on web, iOS, and Android platforms

NoirNourish is perfect for those seeking a straightforward, efficient app to support healthier eating habits.

3. Cronometer

Cronometer is known for its detailed micronutrient tracking, making it ideal for users focused on specific dietary needs. This app includes features like:

  • Micronutrient tracking and daily targets
  • Custom food and recipe additions
  • Exercise and biometric data tracking

Cronometer is available on iOS, Android, and web platforms, with both free and premium versions.

4. Yazio

Yazio is a visually appealing app that combines calorie tracking with meal planning and fasting options. Its beginner-friendly design includes:

  • Intermittent fasting tracker
  • Meal planning with a recipe library
  • Personalized calorie and macronutrient goals

Yazio is available on iOS and Android, offering a premium subscription for additional features.

5. Lose It!

Lose It! is a simple and intuitive app focused on calorie tracking and weight loss goals. It offers:

  • Barcode scanning and food database
  • Customized goals for weight management
  • Community support and challenges

This app is available on iOS, Android, and web, with both free and premium options for advanced features.

6. Lifesum

Lifesum is an all-in-one app that provides meal tracking, meal plans, and diet recommendations. Popular features include:

  • Macro and calorie tracking
  • Diet-specific meal plans (Keto, Vegan, etc.)
  • Integration with fitness trackers

Lifesum is available on iOS and Android, offering a premium subscription for more in-depth insights.

7. MyPlate by LIVESTRONG

MyPlate by LIVESTRONG is a nutrition tracking app that combines food tracking with educational resources to support a healthy lifestyle. Key features include:

  • Comprehensive food and exercise tracker
  • Customizable daily calorie goals
  • Nutrition insights and tips for healthier choices

Available on iOS and Android, MyPlate offers both free and premium options with additional resources.

8. FatSecret

FatSecret is a straightforward app that offers food tracking, exercise logging, and a supportive community. Its features include:

  • Barcode scanner and food diary
  • Weight and progress tracking
  • Community support and recipe ideas

FatSecret is available on iOS, Android, and web, with both free and premium versions.
